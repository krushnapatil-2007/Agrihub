Central Government Schemes

Major flagship initiatives by the Government of India.

PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi

₹6,000 per year income support for all landholding farmers.

PM Fasal Bima Yojana

Comprehensive crop insurance against non-preventable natural risks.

PM Krishi Sinchayee Yojana

"Har Khet Ko Pani" - Subsidies for drip/sprinkler irrigation systems.

e-NAM (National Agri Market)

Pan-India electronic trading portal for better price discovery.

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas

Financial assistance of ₹50,000/ha for organic farming.

PM Kisan Maan-Dhan

Pension scheme for small farmers (₹3000/month after age 60).

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Get short-term credit for crops at low interest rates.

Soil Health Card Scheme

Get soil testing done to know nutrient status and recommendations.

PM-KUSUM Yojana

Subsidies for setting up solar water pumps and solar power plants on barren land.

Sub-Mission on Agri Mechanization

Financial assistance for purchasing tractors, drones, and modern farm machinery.

Agri Infrastructure Fund

Loans with interest subvention for post-harvest management infrastructure.

NMSA (Sustainable Agri)

Promoting climate-resilient farming, water use efficiency, and soil conservation.

Mission for Integrated Horticulture

Holistic growth of the horticulture sector (fruits, vegetables, bamboo, etc.) to increase income.

Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana

Flexible funding for states to plan their own agriculture development programs.

PM Matsya Sampada Yojana

Focused on the sustainable development of the fisheries sector and blue revolution.
